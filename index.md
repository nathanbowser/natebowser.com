# Nate Bowser

Senior Software Engineer, AI Automation and Visualization Systems for managed agents, data-heavy product software, and founder-close product work.

I’m Nate. Twenty years in, mostly building the visualization and dashboard infrastructure behind a long-lived product. Lately: shaping AI agents — Codex, Claude, Cursor, retrieval — into engineering leverage that doesn’t bury the codebase. I work best close to founders, on things that need to feel built, not just shipped. My strongest work has been at Spider Strategies, where I spent 15 years building Dash, Spider Impact, and the D3/SVG/canvas primitives behind charts, gauges, trees, maps, exports, and dashboard editing.

## Focus areas

- Visualization systems: D3, SVG, and canvas components for charts, gauges, trees, maps, exports, and dashboard composition.
- Product engineering: full-stack product work across Spider Impact, Dash, internal CMS tooling, websites, deployment, and browser performance.
- AI automation: managed coding agents, agent-readable corpora, MCP services, review automation, prompt workflows, and codebase-specific engineering tools.
- Developer enablement: enterprise training background, AI-agent workflows, technical exercises, and practical guidance that helps teams move faster without losing code quality.
